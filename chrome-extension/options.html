Where do I find the document id?

  • Install the Android app and scan a QR-Code. The first time you use the app a bezahlScannerData.csv will be created within your Google Drive account.
  • Log in to your Google drive account
  • Right click on the bezahlScannerData file and select 'Link abrufen/Create link'. A popup window will show up.
  • Select the id (all the content after "id=")
